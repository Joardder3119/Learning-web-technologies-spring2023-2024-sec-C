CV Writing Tips

General Tips

Here are some tips for writing a successful CV:

  • Tailor your CV to the job you're applying for
  • Use a clear and professional format
  • Include relevant skills and experiences
  • Highlight your achievements and accomplishments
  • Proofread carefully for errors

Sections to Include

Consider including the following sections in your CV:

  • Contact Information
  • Personal Statement or Objective
  • Work Experience
  • Education
  • Skills
  • Additional Sections (such as Certifications, Volunteer Work, etc.)
